Soup

Soup: It's the easiest way to warm up your kitchen on a cold day and feed yourself and your family in one delicious and healthy bowl. But you don't need a recipe to make soup - especially if you have a clutch of miscellaneous vegetables hanging out in your crisper. Nearly any vegetable can be turned into soup with a little time and effort.

If you are craving soup and you have vegetables and broth in your cupboard, you're all set. Here's a step-by-step guide to transforming nearly any kind of vegetable into delicious, nourishing soup.

Beethoven once said "Only the pure of heart can make a good soup". Well, here's my attempt at doing just that!

List of Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (or another cooking oil such as canola oil or sunflower oil)
  • 1/2 large onion (chopped)
  • 2 ribs celery (chopped)
  • 2 carrots (sliced and peeled)
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (chopped, try cauliflower, zucchini, or green beans)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 8 cups vegetable broth (or 8 cups water + 2 cubes vegetable bouillon)
  • 1 (14-ounce) can tomatoes (undrained)

Steps for making Soup

  1. Heat oil in a large soup or stock pot. Add the chopped onion, optional minced garlic, chopped celery, and sliced carrots. Sauté, stirring for 3 to 4 minutes, until onions are soft but do not let them brown.
  2. Add your choice of mixed chopped vegetables and heat for 1 to 2 minutes more. Do not let them brown.
  3. Add salt, garlic powder, thyme, and oregano stirring to coat the vegetables well.
  4. Add bay leaf, vegetable broth or water mixed with bouillon cubes, and undrained tomatoes. Bring to a boil, stir, reduce heat, and simmer until the vegetables are tender - about 8 to 12 minutes.
  5. Adjust seasonings to taste
  6. Remove the bay leaf and serve your homemade vegetable soup with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese or Parmesan cheese substitute and some crusty bread or crackers.

Salad

A salad is a dish consisting of a mixture of small pieces of food, usually vegetables.However, different varieties of salad may contain virtually any type of ready-to-eat food. Salads are typically served at room temperature or chilled, with notable exceptions such as south German potato salad which is served warm.

Who can resist a fresh salad ! Here's one of my favorites.

List of Ingredients

  • 2 hearts romaine lettuce
  • 2 small plum tomatoes, diced
  • 1 Kirby cucumber or 1/4 European seedless cucumber, diced
  • 1 small yellow onion or 1/2 red onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled and shredded
  • 1/4 cup (a couple of glugs) extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons (a couple of splashes) red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Coarse salt and black pepper

Steps for making Salad

  1. Place salad greens in a salad bowl and top with tomatoes, cucumbers, onion, and carrot.
  2. Place oil, vinegar and sugar in a small plastic container.
  3. Put the lid on the container and shake dressing until sugar dissolves, about 1 minute.
  4. Pour dressing over salad.
  5. Season salad with salt and pepper, to taste, and toss to combine.

Pizza

Pizza is a traditional Italian dish consisting of a yeasted flatbread typically topped with tomato sauce and cheese and baked in an oven. It can also be topped with additional vegetables, meats, and condiments, and can be made without cheese.Pizza is one of the most popular foods in the world and a common fast food item.

List of Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough, at room temperature for 1 hour.
  • 1/2 to 1 cup sauce: tomato sauce, white sauce, or other spread.
  • 2 to 3 cups other toppings: sautéed onions, sautéed mushrooms, pepperoni, cooked sausage, cooked bacon, diced peppers, leftover veggies, or any other favorite toppings.
  • 1 to 2 cups shredded or sliced cheese (4 to 8 ounces) such as mozzarella, Monterey Jack, provolone, fontina, or any other favorite.
  • Cornmeal or all-purpose flour (optional)

Steps for making Pizza

  1. Heat the oven to 550°F or higher.
  2. Divide the dough in half.
  3. Roll out the dough.
  4. Top the pizza.
  5. Bake the pizza.
  6. Slice and serve.
